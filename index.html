Safety Stock Formula

SS = z' · √( σd² · L' + μd² · σL'² )
SS = Safety Stock (units)
z' = Adjusted service factor (includes risk signals)
σ_d = Demand standard deviation
L' = Adjusted lead time (days)
μ_d = Average daily demand
σ_L' = Lead time standard deviation
